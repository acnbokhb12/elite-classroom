Class A

Arisu Sakayanagi

Arisu is introduced in the anime when she and Kōhei Katsuragi were being talked to by the Student Council President about the school system.

After a while, she is seen next to Kōhei Katsuragi in the hallway while observing the class points for each class. She then proceeded by commenting on how things gets very boring due to seemingly no struggle between the groups.

Katsuragi Kohei

Due to his failure during the island exam, his faction loses much influence and eventually succumbs to Arisu Sakayanagi's faction.

Kohei won Honami Ichinose's recognition when she reported him to Chie Hoshinomiya (her teacher).

During the Event Selection Exam, he is selected as one of Class 1-A (1st Year)'s representatives during the math portion.

Class B

Honami Ichinos

Honami is a girl of average height, with blue eyes and blonde sideburns that reach her back. She possesses an extremely slow and bouncy body, making even Suzune Horikita jealous.

Honami is a kind person to everyone, even to the students of the lower grades, unlike most students of the other high ranking classes.

Not much is known about her physical abilities, although the short story section of Volume 3 of the light novel states that she is not very good at sports, given the fact that her balance skills are quite bad.

She is very intelligent as a student in a high-ranking class, and other classmates who joined her study group also passed the test.

Ryūji Kanzaki

- As a student of Class B, Ryūji is intelligent, which is manifested when he recognized the fact that three boys from Class C could have overpowered Ken Sudō due to their athletic abilities.

- Ryūji is first introduced in his class when their teacher Chie Hoshinomiya announced their class grades after a quiz.

- During the survival test, he and Honami went to Class C camp, but they found it deserted. They were joined by Kiyotaka and Airi Sakura who were scouting the forest but ended up at the beach.

Class C

Kakeru Ryūen

Kakeru is an infamous hooligan who has gathered many followers and as the representative of his class, he is the leader of his class ruling them not by loyalty but fear.

In spite of his regular violent actions and tenancies, Kakeru is shown to be someone who is very intelligent as he successfully persuaded and forged an alliance with Kōhei Katsuragi to aid him in the survival test.

Eventually, he was able to convince Kohei to join his class and leave Class A by appealing to.

Mio Ibuki

Mio is shown to be a capable fighter, but she is not as strong as Kakeru Ryūen, who is able to dodge her attacks and overpower her easily. In the special island exam arc, she defeated Suzune Horikita in combat.

After the events of the exam, Kiyotaka states that Mio likely would have won even if Suzune wasn't sick. Mio adopted Capoeira, a Brazilian martial art, as her main fighting style.[6] Despite her skill, she is an inferior opponent against Kiyotaka Ayanokōji, who easily swatted her aside.

Albert Yamada

Due to Albert's large frame, he is a very strong individual, as shown when he was ordered by Kakeru Ryūen to beat Daichi Ishizaki and his friends for their failure which left them battered.

Albert fought alongside Mio Ibuki, Kakeru Ryūen and Daichi Ishizaki but ultimately lost the fight due to Kiyotakas overwhelming strength and experience in martial arts which he attained from the White Room.

Class D

Kiyotaka Ayanokōji

Kiyotaka's past is showered in enigma, but brief glances has shown him to be extremely intelligent.

While Kiyotaka did attempt to make friends when he joined his class, his inexperience in social matters caused him to miss out in forming a group.

This action earned praise from Manabu, who inquired as to what fighting arts he practiced.

Kei Karuizawa

Kei was assessed as being subpar by the school standard. Her frequent unexplained absences and below average grades in middle school were direct reasons for her being placed in Class D. She doesn't like academics and sports, but she is surprisingly able to compete with others in both when she is willing to exert effort.

Kei has picked up on the ability to sense the slightest bits of changes in the atmosphere, expressions and emotions of others.

Kikyō Kushida

Abilities

Kikyō is slightly above the average in the academic field and was able to manipulate many students in her school into believing she was a benevolent presence.

She also exudes one of the highest social contribution scores, showing that despite her surreptitious spiteful personality, she was able to trick the school into believing that her contributions were from charitable desires and not self-centred ones.

She also asked if he had to choose between siding with her or Suzune as he remarked.

very dedicated and diligent in her studies, having nothing else to do. Suzune has proven to be gifted with superior overall abilities as she excels in both academics and physical fields.
